Pho

Description

Pho is a traditional Vietnamese soup made with beef broth, rice noodles, and fresh herbs. It's a comforting and aromatic dish that's perfect for any time of day.

This recipe is simple to prepare and yields a rich, flavorful broth that's perfect for family meals or special occasions. Enjoy the authentic taste of Vietnam in your own kitchen!

Ingredients

  • Rice noodles
  • Beef broth
  • Sliced beef
  • Fresh herbs (cilantro, basil, mint)
  • Bean sprouts
  • Lime wedges
  • Chili peppers

Steps

  1. Prepare the beef broth by simmering beef bones and spices for several hours.
  2. Cook rice noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Thinly slice the beef and set aside.
  4. Place cooked noodles in a bowl and pour hot beef broth over them.
  5. Add sliced beef to the broth and let it cook for a few minutes until done.
  6. Top with fresh herbs, bean sprouts, lime wedges, and chili peppers.
